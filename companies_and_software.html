Google is an advertising company, and Chrome is just another tool to further than objective.

Floc (dead)

FLOC (Federated Learning of Cohorts) was a feature in Chrome, until Google killed it off in 2022. It would put people in "cohorts" based on their interests, and allow websites to request these "cohorts".
